#Greek-Team Udacity Travelling Card Project

Thanks to Udacity and the Google Developer Challenge a lot of different people from different countries and continents have met here and created a wonderful community, so why not share some pictures and basic information about the place we live, historical monuments, sites of archeological interest or simply our favourite travel destinations. Each contributor can post one or multiple cards that will be pinned on the map, by clicking on the marker the card with info will pop up! Sounds fun? welcome aboard ;p


Udacity Travelling Card Project is an open source project made by #team-greek-project community.
